Meo Urban Trail

12 Km on the amazing streets of Oporto.

Imagine a race at night full of lights and adventures. A urban trail across the city of Oporto and Vila Nova de Gaia.

  • Date: October 2014
  • Distance: 12 Km

Porto Marathon

The epic distance across the city of Oporto.

The 11th Edition of Porto Marathon will take place in November 2nd 2014 at 9:00. The start line for the Marathon runners will be at Rua Julio Dinis (next to Palácio de Cristal) and the finish line will be at Av da Boavista (next to Parque da Cidade).

  • Date: November 2014
  • Distance: 42 Km

Family Race

For those that just can't stop running.

On the same day as the Marathon there will be race with 16 km distance, for federated and non-federated athletes born in 1994 and earlier.

  • Date: November 2014
  • Distance: 16 Km
